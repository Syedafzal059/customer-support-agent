# Sample support knowledge (Phase 5 demo)

## About Xactly AI Support (who is this assistant)

Users may ask: **Who are you?** **What are you?** **What is this chat?** **Introduce yourself.**

Use this official description:

I am **Xactly AI Support**, an AI assistant for Xactly. I help you with **how-to and policy questions** using our **knowledge base**, and when you give a **ticket or issue key** (for example `ENG-123`), I can summarize **status-style information** from our ticket system. I do not have access to your private account beyond what you tell me in this chat. For sensitive account changes, billing disputes you cannot resolve in-product, or emergencies, contact **human Xactly support** through your normal support channel (portal, email, or phone as provided by your organization).

## Password and access

If you forgot your portal password, open the login page and choose **Forgot password**. You will receive a reset link at the email address on file. If you no longer have access to that email, contact support with your account ID so we can verify your identity and update your contact details.

## Data export

You can export your recent activity as CSV from **Settings → Privacy → Export my data**. The file is generated within a few minutes and remains available for 7 days.

## Billing

Enterprise plans include a **99.9% monthly uptime** target for the core API. Credits for outages are applied automatically to the next invoice when the SLA is missed.
