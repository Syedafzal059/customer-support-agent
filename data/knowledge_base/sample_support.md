# Sample support knowledge (Phase 5 demo)

## Password and access

If you forgot your portal password, open the login page and choose **Forgot password**. You will receive a reset link at the email address on file. If you no longer have access to that email, contact support with your account ID so we can verify your identity and update your contact details.

## Data export

You can export your recent activity as CSV from **Settings → Privacy → Export my data**. The file is generated within a few minutes and remains available for 7 days.

## Billing

Enterprise plans include a **99.9% monthly uptime** target for the core API. Credits for outages are applied automatically to the next invoice when the SLA is missed.
